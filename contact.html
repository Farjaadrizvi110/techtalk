Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and solutions

What restaurant management solutions do you offer?

We offer comprehensive restaurant management solutions including inventory management, staff scheduling, table reservation systems, and customer relationship management. Our solutions are designed to streamline operations and improve efficiency.

How does virtual assistance work for restaurants?

Our virtual assistance solutions use AI-powered chatbots and virtual assistants to handle customer inquiries, take orders, and provide support 24/7. This reduces the need for additional staff while maintaining high-quality customer service.

Can your AI solutions integrate with existing systems?

Yes, our AI solutions are designed to seamlessly integrate with existing restaurant management systems, EPOS platforms, and other software. We provide custom integration services to ensure compatibility and smooth data flow between systems.

What EPOS systems do you support?

We support a wide range of EPOS systems including TouchBistro, Lightspeed, Toast, Square, and many others. Our solutions can integrate with most major EPOS platforms to provide enhanced functionality and data insights.
